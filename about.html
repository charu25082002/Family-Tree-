Our Story

Founded in 2020, Family Tree Guest House began as a small family home with just 6 rooms, built on the principles of warmth, comfort, and genuine hospitality.

What started as a passion project for the Johnson family has blossomed into a beloved establishment known for its personal touch and exceptional service. Over the years, we've expanded our facilities while maintaining the intimate, family-friendly atmosphere that our guests love.

Today, we continue to be family-owned and operated, with three generations working together to create unforgettable experiences for every guest who walks through our doors.
